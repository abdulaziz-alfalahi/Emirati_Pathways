## рџ“‹ Detailed Feature Requirements

### **1. Family Engagement Interface**

*   **Simplified Dashboard:** A non-professional, intuitive interface designed for parents, focusing on key information and actions.
*   **Child's Profile Overview:** A summary of the child's academic progress, skill development, and career interests.
*   **Communication Hub:** A secure messaging system for parents to communicate with educators and mentors (with the student's consent).
*   **Event Calendar:** A shared calendar for tracking important academic deadlines, career fairs, and school events.

### **2. Educational and Career Tracking**

*   **Real-time Progress Monitoring:** Track academic performance, course completion, and skill acquisition.
*   **Career Pathway Visualization:** A visual representation of potential career paths based on the child's interests and skills.
*   **Educational Opportunity Alerts:** Notifications about relevant courses, workshops, and extracurricular activities.
*   **Goal Setting and Tracking:** A collaborative tool for parents and children to set and monitor educational and career goals.

### **3. Family-Oriented Analytics and Reporting**

*   **Customized Reports:** Generate reports on academic performance, skill development, and career readiness.
*   **Benchmarking:** Compare a child's progress against anonymized peer data.
*   **AI-Powered Insights:** Receive AI-generated recommendations for educational support and career guidance.
*   **Family-Focused KPIs:** Track metrics related to family engagement, student progress, and platform usage.
