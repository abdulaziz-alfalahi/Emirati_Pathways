## Production Guide

### Visual Style
*   **Color Palette**: Primary colors should be teal (`#0d9488`) and dark green (`#00843D`), with accents of gold (`#FFC400`) and the UAE flag colors (red, green, white, black).
*   **Typography**: Use the Dubai font for all on-screen text to maintain brand consistency.
*   **Motion Graphics**: Animations should be smooth, modern, and professional. Use subtle transitions and avoid overly flashy effects.

### Audio
*   **Voiceover**: The narration should be delivered in a clear, confident, and inspiring tone. Consider both English and Arabic voiceovers for localization.
*   **Music**: Use an uplifting and motivational instrumental track that complements the visuals and narration.
*   **Sound Effects**: Use subtle sound effects to enhance animations and transitions.

### Asset Checklist
*   [x] Cinematic aerial footage of Dubai
*   [x] Professional headshots of personas
*   [x] Platform dashboard mockups
*   [x] AI and technology visualizations
*   [x] Infographics and data charts
*   [x] D33 and Talent33 timeline

### Post-Production
*   **Editing**: The video should be edited at a professional level, with seamless transitions, well-paced scenes, and a clear narrative flow.
*   **Color Grading**: Apply color grading to ensure a consistent and visually appealing look throughout the video.
*   **Subtitles**: Add subtitles in both English and Arabic for accessibility and localization.
