## Production Specifications

### Visual Style
- **Color Palette**: UAE flag colors (red, green, white, black) with teal accents
- **Typography**: Dubai Font family throughout
- **Animation Style**: Smooth, professional transitions with subtle motion graphics
- **Branding**: Consistent UAE government visual identity

### Technical Requirements
- **Resolution**: 4K (3840x2160) for future-proofing
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 for web, 9:16 vertical version for mobile
- **Audio**: Professional voiceover with background music
- **Subtitles**: Arabic and English options
- **Accessibility**: Full compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA standards

### Localization
- **Arabic Version**: Complete Arabic voiceover and text
- **Cultural Adaptation**: UAE-specific examples and references
- **RTL Support**: Right-to-left text direction for Arabic content

### Distribution Strategy
- **Primary**: Platform homepage and demo modal
- **Secondary**: Government presentations and conferences
- **Social Media**: Shortened versions for LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram
- **Training**: Internal onboarding and user education
